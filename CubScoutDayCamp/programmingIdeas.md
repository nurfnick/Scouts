# Ideas for Day Camp

## Programming

1. BB Guns
2. Archery
3. Swimming
4. Canoes?
4. Games
    a. Capture the flag (dino)
    b. Dino Hunt (2 scouts in middle big circle eyes closed, one dino one hunter.  Dino tries to escape circle.  Scouts in circle try to figure where dino is.  Cycle with players)
    c. Catch the dinos tail (Divide the Cub Scouts into two teams. They line up single file, holding each other around the waist. The first Cub is the dinosaur's head, and the last Cub is the dinosaur's tail. On signal, the dinosaur's head tries to catch the other dinosaur's tail. The tail tries to keep from being caught. The Cub Scouts must not let go of each other. The longer the body of the dinosaur, the more fun it is to play.)
5. Crafts
    a. Wooden Toys I have
    b. Dino neckerchief slides (silicone mold and wood back)
    c. Branding
6. Rope Bridge
